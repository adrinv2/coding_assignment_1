art map
The modern perception of a painting is in a snapshot. We rush from museum to museum, across cities, within the span of a few minutes. There is no love for the artwork, no patience. A mechanical lense replaces our eyes, dictates how we capture colour, form, dimention. We strive to prove to the world what we have seen by sharing it on social media, altering the purpose of a museum; it is our image archive to solely photograph. An evidence room.

"Visto y no visto"⁠— in the blink of an eye, "to see but not see". This is a museum for those rushing, flashing tourists. This is a museum of the future. Click your ticket to enter.
